About Me, the Developer

I built My Desk out of a personal need for a straightforward, beautiful space to manage my own day-to-day tasks and thoughts. I found that most apps were cluttered and overly complex. My goal was to build a tool that feels like a clean notebook—a digital space where you can focus on what matters most, without any distractions.

Purpose

My Desk was built to be a simple, clean, and distraction-free tool for managing your personal life. We believe productivity should be effortless.

Philosophy

We've adopted a minimalist approach, inspired by the simplicity of a blank notebook page. Every feature is intentional, designed to help you organize your thoughts and tasks efficiently.

Future

This is just the start. We plan to continuously expand My Desk's capabilities, adding new features to help you stay on top of your game.
